\begin{abstract}
We present the HQCB hypothesis: a self-consistent bootstrap linking an evolving effective Higgs vacuum expectation value to cosmological calibration and expansion-rate inference. We provide a minimal effective action, field equations, an LQG/LQC-motivated discretization bridge at the level of effective operators, and a reproducible inference pipeline with uncertainty propagation and model comparison.
\end{abstract}
